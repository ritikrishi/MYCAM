Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my wedding photography?

We recommend booking your wedding photography at least 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season dates (May-October). This ensures availability and gives us ample time for planning.

What is included in your wedding photography packages?

Our wedding packages typically include pre-wedding consultations, full-day coverage, a second photographer, digital images, and an online gallery. We also offer additional services like engagement sessions, albums, and prints.

How long does it take to receive the final photos?

For weddings, delivery time is typically 6-8 weeks. For engagement sessions and other shoots, delivery time is usually 2-3 weeks.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes, we love to travel! Additional travel fees may apply depending on the location. Please contact us for a custom quote for your destination wedding.
